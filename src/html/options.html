Internal Keyboard/Mouse Shortcuts

You're using URL Plus's internal shortcuts. Click on the text boxes and press the key(s) you'd like to set. Any combination of the Alt, Ctrl, Shift, and Meta modifier keys can be used. If Quick is enabled, you can Plus [+] or Next [>] instantly without having to enter setup.
